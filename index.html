About Zhang

Zhang(M.A.) is an incoming Ph.D. student at VIVA research, University of Ottawa. Before admission to uOttawa, he served as data scientist intern at Flipboard China. In addition, he is a senior visiting scholar in Dept.GSC at the United Nations Italy. He is dedicated to 3D detection research for autonomous driving. He cares about minority rights. He was very fond of Beethoven's classical symphonies.
Zhang在加拿大渥太华大学取得了M.A.学位，并即将继续攻读Ph.D.。在进入渥太华大学之前，他是Flipboard中国的Data Scientist NLP。他致力于自动驾驶的3D检测研究。除此以外，他还是中国的高级访问学者。他曾在意大利的联合国GSC中心供职GIS Administrator，关心少数族裔权利和民主。在业余时间，他很喜欢风景摄影和欣赏大编制交响乐，但在风格上不拘古典与现代。他喜欢兔子，龙猫，小香猪但从未饲养过。
