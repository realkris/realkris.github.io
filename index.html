About Zhang

Zhang(M.A.) is about to become a PhD student at the University of Ottawa. In addition, he is a senior visiting scholar in GIS at the United Nations. He is dedicated to 3D inspection research for autonomous driving. At the same time, he is a staff member of the United Nations, GIS Administrator at the United Nations Global Service Centre. He is also a contributor to Vision China. He cares about minority rights and democracy while loving Totoro.
Zhang即将成为渥太华大学的全奖博士生。他致力于自动驾驶的3D检测研究。除此以外，他还是中国的高级访问学者。同时，他也是联合国的工作人员，在United Nations Global Service Centre任职GIS Administrator。他也是视觉中国的供稿人。他在喜欢龙猫的同时关心少数族裔权利和民主。
